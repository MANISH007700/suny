## Automatic vs Manual Escalation

### Automatic Escalation
- System detects sensitive keywords (financial aid, failing, etc.)
- AI expresses uncertainty or lack of information
- Happens WITHOUT student action
- Creates escalation automatically

### Manual Escalation (NEW!)
- **Student initiates** by clicking button
- Student fills out detailed form
- Student controls priority and reason
- Student adds specific context

### Both Types:
- Go to same advisor dashboard
- Handled by advisors the same way
- Include full conversation history
- Track student profile and risk

## Escalation Form Fields Explained

### Your Question
- **Pre-filled**: Last question you asked
- **Editable**: Add more context or clarify
- **Required**: Must have content

### AI Response
- **Pre-filled**: What AI said
- **Editable**: You can modify or add why it wasn't helpful
- **Optional**: Can be left blank for manual escalations

### Reason Dropdown
- **Purpose**: Helps route to appropriate specialist
- **Required**: Must select one
- **Tip**: If none fit exactly, choose "Other" and explain in notes

### Urgency Slider
- **Range**: 1 (low) to 5 (critical)
- **Default**: 2 (normal)
- **Impact**: Higher priority = faster advisor response
- **Be honest**: Don't inflate urgency artificially

### Additional Context
- **Optional**: But highly recommended
- **Purpose**: Give advisor full picture
- **Include**: Deadlines, special circumstances, previous attempts
- **Length**: No limit, be as detailed as needed

## FAQ

**Q: Can I escalate after the conversation is over?**
A: The button appears when there are messages in your conversation. If you closed the chat, just ask the question again and then escalate.

**Q: How do I know if my escalation was received?**
A: You'll see a confirmation message with an escalation ID. Keep this ID for your records.

**Q: Can I cancel an escalation?**
A: Once submitted, escalations cannot be canceled by students. Contact the advisor if no longer needed.

**Q: Will I be notified when an advisor responds?**
A: (This depends on your notification system - typically yes via email)

**Q: Can I escalate multiple questions?**
A: Yes! Each question can be escalated separately with its own form.

**Q: What if I mark the wrong priority?**
A: Advisors can adjust priority if needed. Don't worry too much about getting it perfect.

**Q: Is there a limit to escalations?**
A: No hard limit, but please escalate only when truly needed.

**Q: Can I see my escalation status?**
A: Currently only advisors see the dashboard. (Feature could be added for students to track their own escalations)
